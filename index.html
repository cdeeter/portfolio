about
Hello! My name is Celia and I'm a Front End Developer from Oakland, CA.

In 2013, I graduated with a B.A. in Psychology from San Francisco State. After over a year of working in Special Education, the Psychology field no longer seemed like a good fit for me so I decided to change careers. While weighing my options, I realized that one of my favorite hobbies as a kid, making websites, was actually a career option! In summer of 2014 I taught myself how to code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and found my true passion!

Getting into Web Development helped me find a creative side of myself that I never knew I had. I became addicted to making stuff, solving problems, and learning new technologies. Teaching myself everything takes a lot of dedication, persistence, and self-motivation. I've developed a passion for JavaScript and am working to become a Fullstack JavaScript programmer. In addition to Web Development, I'm also interested in video games, technology, music, comics, and social justice.
